If your daughter is suddenly more withdrawn, anxious, emotional, or just not herself… you’re not alone.

  • Open up more easily and communicate with clarity
  • Feel safe, seen, and supported—without judgment

Why Big Sister?

Founded by Dawn Davis Lawrence, a specialist coach with years of experience mentoring teenage girls, Big Sister offers a warm, personalised approach that connects with girls on a real level—no lectures, no pressure, just honest, empowering support.
